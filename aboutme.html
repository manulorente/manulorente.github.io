I started my career at Anafocus, working on image sensor development, designing test boards, and automating measurements. Later, I moved abroad to work with e2v in France and China, where I focused on camera middleware and system development. Now, as an Application Engineer at Teledyne-e2v in Denver, I manage technical support, product development, and client relationships. My work involves coding drivers, creating APIs, and assisting clients with complex issues.

While studying Telecommunications Engineering at the University of Seville, I was balancing studies with national rowing competitions. After deciding to focus on my career, I entered the semiconductor industry and completed my degree. I later started a Master in Electronics and Embedded Systems at University of Malaga, to bridge knowledge gaps in electronics. This motivated me into software and cloud computing, leading me to another Master in Cloud Native Applications Development at the University Rey Juan Carlos.

My goal is to combine my experience in business and applications with my background specializes in IT, cloud computing and software. I hold certifications like LFCA, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Azure Fundamentals, and I'm currently preparing for advanced certifications in Linux, AWS, and Kubernetes to further develop my skills.

Outside of work, I maintain an active lifestyle, enjoying rowing as a hobby, running, hiking, and snowboarding in the Rockies. Staying active helps me balance my personal and professional life.
