I began my career at Anafocus, working on image sensors, designing tests, and automating measurements for characterization. Later, after the company acquisition by Teledyne Technologies, I moved abroad to join Teledyne-e2v, where I focused on camera middleware and system development. Currently, as Application Engineer I manage technical support, product development, and client relationships. My work involves coding PoCs, creating APIs, implementing CI/CD, training customers, and helping them with the integration and success of our products.

Obtained my degree in Telecommunications Engineering at the University of Seville, and balancing my studies with national rowing competitions. After deciding to focus on my career, I applied to the semiconductor industry filling the space that competitive rowing had left in my life and completing my degree. Later, I started a Master in Electronics and Embedded Systems at University of Malaga, to bridge knowledge gaps in electronics, particularly in embedded systems and low-level programming. This motivated me into software roles, leading me to a second Master in Cloud Native Applications Development at the University Rey Juan Carlos where I delved into cloud computing, microservices, and DevOps culture.

My daily goal is to combine my experience in product and applications development dealing with customers along with my academic background specialized in IT, cloud computing and software. I hold certifications like LFCA, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Azure Fundamentals, and I'm currently preparing for advanced certifications in Linux, AWS, and Kubernetes to further develop my skills.

Grew up in Seville, Spain, moved to Grenoble, France during my studies in 2017, and later to Shenzhen, China after undergrad in 2018. In 2019, I returned to Seville, Spain and have recently relocated to Denver, Colorado in 2024.

Outside of work, I maintain an active lifestyle, enjoying rowing as a hobby, as well as running, soccer, and snowboarding in the Rockies. Staying active helps me balance my personal and professional life.
